The efimer

Idea

Efimer is a project based on the concept that fashion is ephemeral. To represent this idea I designed a haute couture collection made dy vegetables, wich are ephemeral too. I decided to paint the vegetables to give uniformity at the clothes and express the purity that provides the white color.
